MyMovieList

A movie watchlist management system

Purpose

Purpose of the System

MyMovieList is a web-based movie watchlist management system designed to help movie enthusiasts track, organize, and rate their movie-watching experience. Inspired by platforms like AniList and Letterboxd, it provides a clean and intuitive interface to manage your personal film journey.

Whether you're a casual viewer or a dedicated cinephile, MyMovieList helps you keep track of what you're watching, what you've completed, and what you plan to watch next—all powered by live data from The Movie Database (TMDB) API.

Inspiration

This project was heavily inspired by AniList, One of the most popular anime listing websites. The dark UI, cover grid layout, hover previews, and sidebar filter design are all adapted from AniList's clean aesthetic.
